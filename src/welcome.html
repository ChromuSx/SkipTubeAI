rocket_launch Quick Setup Guide

Follow these simple steps to get started:

  1. Get an API Key: Sign up on Anthropic Console or OpenAI Platform and generate your API key.
  2. Configure the extension: Click the SkipTube AI icon in your browser toolbar and enter your API key in the "API Configuration" section.
  3. Customize your preferences: Select which content types you want to skip (sponsorships, intros, outros, donations, self-promotions) and adjust advanced settings.
  4. Start watching: Open any YouTube video with subtitles and the extension will automatically analyze and skip unwanted segments!

Note: Your API key is stored locally on your device and only used to communicate with your selected AI provider. The extension only works with videos that have subtitles/transcripts available.
